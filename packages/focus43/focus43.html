Apps, apps, apps!

There's a lot to consider in the process of building an app that will be a value add to your business: What should the app do? Where should the content come from? Is an app what you really need, or should you consider updating your website to work on mobile browsers? Who will use the app?

At Focus43, you'll get the help you need to make good decisions.

Your Website

Used to be you just built a website for your average browser, and that was the end of it. That is no longer the truth. With all the different devices used for browsing, there are many more factors to consider: Do you care about a mobile browser experience? If so, what does that entail? Should you have a mobile specific site, or should you go with a responsive design?

Focus43 will help you understand your options, and create the product that will be most valuable for you.

MVP

Minimum Viable Product. Let's face it, everyone has a budget. Does that mean you have to settle for a sub-par experience? Not necessarily! A big part of building an application, is digging in and really understanding what your application needs to do. Adding tons of functionality, until you know it's needed, could actually disintegrate the user experience.

Focus43 will challenge you to think about what kind of application will add the most value to your business.
